PROJECTS

Here are our recent and successful projects, including web applications, mobile games and mobile apps. These projects demonstrates our expertise in various programming languages and frameworks, highlighting the quality of our work and ability to deliver top-notch software solutions.

Aston Fitness

Aston Fitness is a full-stack web application that allows users to sign up and sign in with Aston Sports Center Gym, users can book classes, join fitness challenges with other users and track their fitness progress. The app is built using React, Node.js, Express, and MongoDB.

Car and Track

Car and Track is a multiplayer car racing mobile game that allows up to 5 users to race against each other over Bluetooth, WiFi or challenge other players online. The game is built using Unity and C#.

Track Navigator

Track Navigator is a mobile app that allows users to search for and view information about different tracks on their personal playlists from major music streaming services i.e Spotify, Youtube Music, Amazon Music and Apple Music. The app is built using React and Firebase.
